Remove a bookmark from an article.  The article will no longer appear
in your bookmarked list.

EXAMPLES
  # Remove a bookmark
  peerpedia bookmark remove abc12345

  # Remove by title keyword
  peerpedia bookmark remove "radioactivity"

This only removes your bookmark — it does not delete the article.
The article remains available; you just won't see it in --bookmarked.

SEE ALSO
  bookmark add       Save an article for later
  article list --bookmarked   See your bookmarked articles
