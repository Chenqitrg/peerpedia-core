Recover your signing key from your password and salt.  Use this after
"account bootstrap" to verify your password and load your session, or
if your session file was lost.

EXAMPLES
  # Recover by name
  peerpedia account recover --name "Marie Curie"

  # Recover by user ID (if multiple users share the name)
  peerpedia account recover --user-id abc12345

You will be prompted for your password.  PeerPedia uses scrypt to
re-derive your Ed25519 key from the password and salt.  If the
derived key matches the stored public key, your session is loaded.

SEE ALSO
  account bootstrap  Set up your identity on a new device
  account whoami     See your current session
  account register   Create a new identity
