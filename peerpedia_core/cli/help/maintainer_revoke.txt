Revoke (withdraw) your consent to publish or merge a paper.  If you
previously consented via "maintainer consent", this reverses it.

EXAMPLES
  # Revoke your consent
  peerpedia maintainer revoke abc12345

You might revoke consent if new issues are found, if you disagree
with co-authors about readiness, or if you want more time to review
changes.  All maintainers must consent for the paper to proceed.

SEE ALSO
  maintainer consent Give your consent to publish
  maintainer list    See consent status of all co-authors
  article publish    Publish when all have consented
