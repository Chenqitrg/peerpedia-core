QUERY PATTERNS
  # Browse all public articles
  peerpedia article list

  # Your own work — including private drafts
  peerpedia article list --mine

  # Your drafts only
  peerpedia article list --mine --status draft

  # Articles from people you follow
  peerpedia article list --feed

  # Search by title or abstract keyword
  peerpedia article list --search "quantum"

  # Filter by status (published, sedimentation, or draft)
  peerpedia article list --status published
  peerpedia article list --status sedimentation

  # Your bookmarked articles
  peerpedia article list --bookmarked

  # Fetch a remote user's articles from a peer server
  peerpedia article list --user <user-id> --server https://peer.example.com

COMBINING FILTERS
  Filters are AND-ed.  Combine them to narrow results:

  # My articles about quantum computing
  peerpedia article list --mine --search "quantum"

  # Published articles from people I follow, about ML
  peerpedia article list --feed --status published --search "machine learning"

Drafts are private — omit --mine to see only public articles.
