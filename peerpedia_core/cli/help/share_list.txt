View your share feed or the articles you've shared.  Shares are
recommendations — when you share a paper, your followers see it.

EXAMPLES
  # See what others have shared (your feed)
  peerpedia share list
  # → Shows recent shares from people you follow

  # See your own shares
  peerpedia share list --mine
  # → Shows articles you've shared

Shares can include an optional comment explaining why you recommend
the paper.  Sharing is a lightweight way to curate reading for your
followers without writing a full review.

SEE ALSO
  share add          Share an article
  share remove       Remove a share
  article list --feed   See articles from followed users
