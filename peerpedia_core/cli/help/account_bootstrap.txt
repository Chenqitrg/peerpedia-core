EXAMPLES
  # On your original device, export your identity:
  peerpedia account whoami --verbose --json

  # Copy the JSON blob to your new device, then:
  peerpedia account bootstrap --from '<paste JSON here>'

  # Verify your password and load the session:
  peerpedia account recover --user-id <your-id>

Bootstrap creates a minimal user stub on a new device so you can
recover your signing key with your password.  After bootstrap,
run "account recover" to verify.

SEE ALSO
  account whoami --verbose    Export your identity
  account recover             Re-derive your key after bootstrap
