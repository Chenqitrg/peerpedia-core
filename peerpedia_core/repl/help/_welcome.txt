[bold info]Welcome to PeerPedia REPL[/] — your interactive research terminal.
[muted]Type a command and press [/][bold]Enter[/][muted] to run it.  Here is how to get started:[/]

[accent]  register --name "Marie Curie"[/]     Create your identity
[accent]  create --title "My Paper"[/]         Write a new paper
[accent]  list[/]                              Browse all papers
[accent]  show "quantum"[/]                    Find and read by title keyword
[accent]  :help create[/]                      See detailed help with examples
