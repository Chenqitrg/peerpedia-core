Walk the social graph to discover new articles.  Starting from users you
follow, PeerPedia looks at who they follow, fetches their articles, and
continues outward up to a set depth.

EXAMPLES
  # Discover articles one hop away (default depth = 1)
  peerpedia sync discover

  # Go deeper into the network
  peerpedia sync discover --depth 3

  # Limit the number of users to traverse
  peerpedia sync discover --depth 2 --max-users 50

This is like "exploring the neighborhood" of your academic network.
For each discovered user, their public articles are pulled and merged
into your local database.  Discovery requires a configured peer server.
