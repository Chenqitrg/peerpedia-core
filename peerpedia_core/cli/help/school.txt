EXAMPLES
  # Fetch from the peer server (default)
  peerpedia school

  # From a specific server
  peerpedia school --server https://peer.example.com

  # Show top 10
  peerpedia school --limit 10

  # Read from local DB only
  peerpedia school --local

The school lists users ranked by follower count.  Follow the top users
to bootstrap your feed — they act as hubs, curating high-quality content
and connecting you to more of the network.

SEE ALSO
  follow             Follow a user from the school
  article list --feed    See what your followed users are reading
  account search     Search for a specific user
