Create your PeerPedia identity.  You only need to do this once.
Your name is how other researchers will find and cite you.

EXAMPLES
  # Register with your real name
  peerpedia account register --name "Marie Curie"

  # Use a shorter name if you prefer
  peerpedia account register --name "M. Curie"

PeerPedia derives a signing key from your password using scrypt.
This key signs every paper and review you create — it proves your
work is really yours.  Keep your password safe.  There is no
password reset.  If you lose it, you must re-register.

SEE ALSO

  account whoami     See your current identity
