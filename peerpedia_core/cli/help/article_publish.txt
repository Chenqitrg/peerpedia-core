EXAMPLES
  # Publish with self-review scores (all 5 dimensions, 1-5)
  peerpedia article publish abc12345 \
      --scores "orig=4,rigor=3,comp=4,ped=3,imp=3"

Publishing moves your article into the 3-day sedimentation pool.
During this period, peers can review it.  After 3 days, run
"article scan" (or wait for the auto-scan) to promote it to published.

Scoring dimensions:
  orig    originality    – novelty of the idea
  rigor   rigor          – methodological soundness
  comp    completeness   – coverage of edge cases
  ped     pedagogy       – clarity of exposition
  imp     impact         – potential significance

Prerequisites: you must be logged in, be a maintainer of the article,
and provide self-review scores.
