Accept a review invitation.  After accepting, you can submit your review.

EXAMPLES
  # Accept a review invitation
  peerpedia review accept abc12345

  # Then submit your review
  peerpedia review submit abc12345 --scores "..." --comment "..."

You can only accept invitations for papers in the sedimentation pool.
Check your notifications to see pending invitations:
  peerpedia notifications

SEE ALSO
  review decline     Decline an invitation
  review submit      Submit your review after accepting
  notifications      Check for pending invitations
