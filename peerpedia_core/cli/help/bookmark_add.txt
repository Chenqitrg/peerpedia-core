Save a paper to read later.  Bookmarked papers appear in your
personal reading list (article list --bookmarked).

EXAMPLES
  # Bookmark a paper
  peerpedia bookmark add abc12345

  # Bookmark by title keyword
  peerpedia bookmark add "Radioactivity"

  # See your bookmarks
  peerpedia article list --bookmarked

Bookmarks are private — only you can see your bookmark list.
If the paper is only on a peer server (not on your machine),
PeerPedia pulls its metadata automatically so you can at least
see the title, author, and abstract.

SEE ALSO
  bookmark remove         Remove a bookmark
  article list --bookmarked   See your bookmarked papers
  article show            Read a paper
