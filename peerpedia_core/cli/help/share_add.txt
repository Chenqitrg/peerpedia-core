Share a paper with your followers.  Sharing is a lightweight way to
recommend something worth reading — your followers will see it in their
feed.  Unlike a review, a share can be as brief as you like.

EXAMPLES
  # Share publicly (all your followers see it)
  peerpedia share add abc12345 --comment "Must read for anyone in the field!"

  # Share to a specific colleague
  peerpedia share add abc12345 --to @marie

  # Share by title keyword
  peerpedia share add "Radioactivity" --comment "Fascinating results"

  # Share without a comment
  peerpedia share add abc12345

WHEN TO SHARE
  • You found an interesting paper others should see.
  • You want to recommend a paper to a specific colleague.
  • You want to highlight a paper's importance without writing a full review.

SEE ALSO
  share list         See shares from people you follow
  share remove       Remove a share you posted
  review submit      Write a full review (more detailed than a share)
