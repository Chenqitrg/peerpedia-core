EXAMPLES
  # Share publicly (visible to your followers)
  peerpedia share add abc12345 --comment "Must read!"

  # Share to a specific user
  peerpedia share add abc12345 --to @bob

Shares appear in your followers' feeds.  Share to a specific user
with --to (by @name, alias, or UUID).

SEE ALSO
  share list         See your shares or your feed
  share remove       Un-share an article
