Browse, search, and filter papers.  This is your main way to discover
what's on PeerPedia — from your own drafts to published work by others.

EXAMPLES
  # All public papers
  peerpedia article list

  # Your own papers (including private drafts)
  peerpedia article list --mine

  # Your drafts only
  peerpedia article list --mine --status draft

  # Papers from researchers you follow (your personal reading list)
  peerpedia article list --feed

  # Search by title keyword
  peerpedia article list --search "quantum"

  # Filter by status
  peerpedia article list --status published       # finished papers
  peerpedia article list --status sedimentation   # papers under review

  # Your bookmarked papers
  peerpedia article list --bookmarked

  # Find a specific researcher's papers (requires a peer server)
  peerpedia article list --user abc12345 --server https://peer.example.com

COMBINING FILTERS
  You can combine multiple filters to narrow results:

  # Your drafts that mention quantum
  peerpedia article list --mine --status draft --search "quantum"

  # Published papers from your feed about machine learning
  peerpedia article list --feed --status published --search "machine learning"

UNDERSTANDING PAPER STATUS
  draft         — private, only you and co-authors can see it
  sedimentation — public review period (3 days, then auto-publishes)
  published     — permanent and citable
