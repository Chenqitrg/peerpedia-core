Set up the same PeerPedia identity on a new device.  This creates a
minimal user record on the new machine so you can recover your signing
key with your password.

EXAMPLES
  # Step 1: On your original device, export your identity
  peerpedia account whoami --verbose --json

  # Step 2: Copy the JSON output to your new device, then:
  peerpedia account bootstrap --from '{"user_id":"abc12345-...","name":"Marie Curie",...}'

  # Step 3: Verify your password and load your session
  peerpedia account recover --user-id abc12345

  # Optional: Connect to a peer server for data sync
  peerpedia account bootstrap --from '<json>' --peer https://peer.example.com

After bootstrap, your new device has a user stub but no papers.
Run "sync pull" to download your papers from a peer server.

SEE ALSO
  account whoami --verbose    Export your identity
  account recover             Verify password after bootstrap
  sync pull                   Download your papers
