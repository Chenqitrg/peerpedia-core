  # Fork a published article to start your own version
  peerpedia fork abc12345

Only published articles can be forked.  The fork starts as a draft
with the original's full git history.  You become the fork's maintainer.

After editing your fork, use "merge propose" to suggest merging
your changes back into the original.

SEE ALSO
  merge propose      Propose merging your fork back
  article edit       Revise your fork
  article list       Find articles to fork
