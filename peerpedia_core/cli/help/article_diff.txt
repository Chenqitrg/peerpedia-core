EXAMPLES
  # Compare the last two commits
  peerpedia diff abc12345 ~1 HEAD

  # Compare two specific commits
  peerpedia diff abc12345 abc12345 def67890

Valid commit refs: a full hash, a short prefix, HEAD, or ~N
(e.g. ~1 for the parent commit, ~3 for three commits back).
