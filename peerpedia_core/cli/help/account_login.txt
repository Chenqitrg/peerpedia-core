Sign in to PeerPedia.  Your password re-derives your signing key.
After logging in, you can write, edit, publish, and review papers.

EXAMPLES
  # Basic login
  peerpedia account login --name "Marie Curie"

  # Login with disambiguation (if multiple users share your name)
  peerpedia account login --name "Marie Curie" --user-id abc12345

  # Login on a new device (fetches your identity from a peer server)
  peerpedia account login --name "Marie Curie" \
      --peer https://peer.example.com --user-id abc12345

If you get "wrong password", check your spelling and try again.
Passwords are case-sensitive.  If you forgot your password entirely,
you must re-register — there is no password reset.

SEE ALSO

  account recover    Restore your key from password + salt
  account whoami     See your current session
  account bootstrap  Alternative way to set up a new device
