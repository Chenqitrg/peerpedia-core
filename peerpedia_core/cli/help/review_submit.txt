SCORING DIMENSIONS
  orig     originality    – novelty of the idea or approach
  rigor    rigor          – methodological soundness
  comp     completeness   – coverage of edge cases and limitations
  ped      pedagogy       – clarity of exposition
  imp      impact         – potential significance to the field

Each dimension is scored 1 (poor) through 5 (exceptional).

EXAMPLES
  # Review a paper in the sedimentation pool
  peerpedia review submit abc12345 \
      --scores "orig=4,rigor=3,comp=4,ped=3,imp=5" \
      --comment "This paper presents a compelling argument..."

  # List reviews you've received
  peerpedia review list abc12345

  # Reply to a reviewer's comment
  peerpedia review reply abc12345 --to @reviewer_name
