Accept a merge proposal — apply someone's fork changes into your paper.
This is how collaborative editing works: someone forks your paper, makes
improvements, proposes a merge, and you accept it.

EXAMPLES
  # Accept a merge proposal
  peerpedia merge accept proposal-1 --target abc12345

Only the target paper's maintainers can accept merge proposals.
If there are conflicts (both you and the fork changed the same part),
the command reports them without applying changes — you'll need to
resolve conflicts manually.

SEE ALSO
  merge propose      Propose a merge from your fork
  merge withdraw     Withdraw a merge proposal you made
  fork               Fork a paper to start editing
